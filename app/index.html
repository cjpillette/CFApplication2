My beginning as a web developer... or

The story of a curious chick

This is my story, the story of a curious chick who stumbled onto coding by accident. That journey started out of the necessity to come up with a system to facilitate my day to day work. I needed to create a custom-tailored tool that would allow me to record, query and graph my data, giving me real-time feedback of the situation. To get the job done, I set out to learn Ruby on Rails. I spent hours trying to grasp foreign concepts: class, function, method, instance variable, schema, migration, relational database... I naively started with not knowing what I didn’t know and slowly discovered the actual extent of what I didn’t know! I just had to be comfortable with the feeling of being uncomfortable, and stick with it!

On the occasions when I would find myself throwing my wings in the air and squawking ‘a-ha, I get it now’, I would immediately wonder ‘yes, but what if...’ and altogether push the door to yet other new worlds...This is exactly how node.js come into the picture!

That’s when I realised that I needed to join a coding bootcamp, so that I would learn more, learn well, learn faster and most importantly learn with other like minded chicks!
