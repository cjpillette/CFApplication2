My beginning as a web developer... or

The story of a curious chick

This is my story, the story of a curious chick who stumbled onto coding by accident. That journey started out of the necessity to come up with a system to facilitate my day to day work. I needed to create a custom-tailored tool that would allow me to record, query and graph my data, giving me real-time feedback of the situation.

To get the job done, I set out to learn Ruby on Rails. I spent hours trying to grasp foreign concepts: class, function, method, instance variable, schema, migration, relational database... I started with not knowing what I didn’t know and soon enough discovered the actual extent of what I didn’t know! I just had to be comfortable with the feeling of being uncomfortable, and stick with it!

On the occasions when I would find myself throwing my wings in the air and happily squawking ‘a-ha, I get it now’, I would immediately wonder ‘yes, but what if...’ and altogether push the door to yet other new worlds...This is exactly how node.js come into the picture!

That’s when I realised that I needed to join a coding bootcamp, so that I would learn more, learn well, learn faster and most importantly learn with other like minded chicks!

Life after Bootcamp

Git branch

When I’m finished with my bootcamp I’m planning on giving back to the coding community. I intend to participate in workshops, seminars, talks and conferences. I want to inspire people and share my knowledge with others.

I can say from experience that sharing your passion, no matter the passion, is the best gift one can offer. It empowers people to change their life for the better.

Git checkout master

On a more personal note, I’d like to work on exciting projects, mostly oriented towards sustainability and reducing our footprint on our environment.

I have the propendency to move around which probably stems from the time when I was a little girl and travelled the world with my family. I love to experience and discover new cultures, see the same thing from different angles.

I’m sincerely convinced that coding will fulfil my life on all these levels.

So I can't wait for this journey to continue !
